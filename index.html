AI-Powered Tomato Leaf Disease Detection

Upload a photo of any tomato leaf and our trained computer vision model instantly identifies disease spots, names the infection, and helps farmers take action before it's too late.

Try the Demo

Why This Matters

Tomato diseases destroy over 30% of global tomato yields annually. Early detection is the key to saving crops.

🌾

30% Crop Loss

Undetected diseases spread rapidly through farms, destroying entire harvests before farmers can react.

👁️

Hard to Spot Early

Many diseases look identical to the naked eye in early stages, leading to wrong treatments.

⏱️

Slow Expert Diagnosis

Sending samples to labs takes days. By then, the disease has spread to neighboring plants.

🤖

AI Can Help Instantly

Our model analyzes leaf photos in seconds, spots diseases humans miss, and suggests the disease name.
